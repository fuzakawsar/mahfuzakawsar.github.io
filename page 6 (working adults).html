Fake Delivery SMS

This scam pretends to be a delivery service you interact with regularly. The message claims there is an issue with your parcel, such as a missed delivery or incomplete address, and pressures you to act quickly.

Clicking the link may lead to a fake website designed to collect personal or payment details. What seems like a small inconvenience can quickly turn into financial loss or identity theft.

Job Offer Email

This scam targets people looking for income, especially students or those under financial pressure.

The email appears casual and appealing, offering flexible work, fast payment, and little experience required.

Excitement or relief can lower suspicion, making victims more likely to comply.

Over time, they may be asked for personal details, upfront fees, or to move money, leading to financial loss, identity theft, or unintended involvement in illegal activity.
